# Secretari User Guide

Welcome to Secretari (Bounny), your AI-powered personal assistant for speech recognition and intelligent memo creation.

## Getting Started

### First Launch
1. **Grant Permissions**: When prompted, allow microphone and speech recognition access
2. **Select Language**: Go to Settings ⚙️ and choose your preferred language for speech recognition
3. **Start Recording**: Tap the red "Start" button to begin your first recording

### Language Setup
**Important**: You must select the correct language in Settings before using speech recognition, otherwise the app won't work properly.

Supported languages:
- English
- Spanish (Latin America)
- Chinese (Simplified)
- Chinese (Traditional)
- Japanese
- Korean

## How to Use

### Recording Speech
1. **Start Recording**: Tap the red "Start" button
2. **Speak Clearly**: Talk normally - the app will transcribe your speech in real-time
3. **Stop Recording**: Tap "Stop" when you're finished
4. **View Results**: Your transcription and AI-generated summary will appear

### Viewing Your Records
- **List View**: See all your recordings in chronological order (newest first)
- **Tap Any Record**: Open detailed view with full transcription and AI summaries
- **Swipe to Delete**: Remove records you no longer need

### AI Processing
After recording, the app can generate:
- **Summaries**: General overview of your speech
- **Checklists**: Actionable items extracted from your speech
- **Translations**: Convert your text to other languages

To process with AI:
1. Open a record from the list
2. Tap "Generate Summary" or "Generate Memo"
3. Wait for AI processing (requires internet connection)
4. View the results

## Troubleshooting

### Speech Recognition Not Working
1. **Check Permissions**: Ensure microphone and speech recognition permissions are granted
2. **Language Settings**: Verify correct language is selected in Settings
3. **Internet Connection**: Some languages require internet for recognition
4. **Device Issues**: Try restarting the app or device

### AI Processing Issues
1. **Internet Connection**: AI processing requires internet connectivity
2. **Account Balance**: Check if you have sufficient credits or active subscription
3. **Server Issues**: Backend services may be temporarily unavailable
4. **Try Again**: Retry processing after a few minutes

### App Performance
1. **Close Other Apps**: Free up memory by closing unnecessary apps
2. **Restart App**: Force close and reopen the app
3. **Update iOS**: Ensure you're running the latest iOS version
4. **Contact Support**: Reach out if issues persist

### Data Issues
1. **Sync Problems**: Check internet connection for account sync
2. **Missing Records**: Verify you're logged into the correct account
3. **Storage Space**: Ensure device has sufficient storage
4. **App Updates**: Keep the app updated to the latest version

### Common Questions

**Q: Does the app record my conversations?**
A: No, the app only processes speech in real-time for transcription. No audio is stored.

**Q: Can I use the app offline?**
A: Speech recognition and AI processing require internet connection. Basic app functions work offline.

**Q: How do I change the language?**
A: Go to Settings and select your preferred language from the language picker.

**Q: Can I export my records?**
A: Currently, records are stored locally on your device. Export functionality may be added in future updates.

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?**
A: All local data will be deleted. Make sure to sync important records to your account.
